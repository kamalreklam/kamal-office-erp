Mobile Navigation Pattern

How should the mobile app navigate between sections?

A

Bottom Tab Bar (iOS/Android style)

Fixed tabs at the bottom: Dashboard, Inventory, Invoices, Clients, More. The "More" tab opens remaining pages. Most natural for phone users — thumb-friendly, instant switching.
